Core Message
One sentence to remember

Applied AI,
built to be used.

Sudais Khalid is an AI/ML engineer and community builder based in Islamabad, Pakistan. His work centers on machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and NLP, turning research-grade AI into products designed to be used, not just demonstrated.

He believes the real work in AI isn't the model; it's everything around it: the pipeline that holds up under real data, the deployment that survives contact with real users, the community that keeps learning after the workshop ends. As originator of the AI Innovation Society, he has spent as much energy building the room where others learn AI as he has building his own systems.
